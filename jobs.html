Software Tester

Melbourne CBD, Victoria ($70,000)

Testing and Quality Assurance

Applicant is responsible for designing, executing, and reporting on software testing activities to ensure that applications function correctly and meet business requirements. They collaborate closely with developers and other stakeholders to identify and document bugs, perform various types of testing (such as functional, regression, and user acceptance testing), and help drive quality improvements in the software development lifecycle. Key responsibilities include creating test plans, writing test cases, automating tests where applicable, and ensuring that any defects are resolved before the software is released to end-users.

Responsibilities include:

  • Timely completion of tasks with a concentrated focus on quality output.
  • Quality checks across a range of digital performance solutions.
  • Check campaigns and complete a detailed checklist across each product.
  • Quality checking of client products leaving no stone unturned, no testing incomplete and be proud to put your name on our solutions in the marketplace.
  • Checking our digital performance campaigns with a fine-toothed comb, to ensure we have their goals and set up our clients for success.
  • Perform comprehensive QA testing on SEO strategies and implementations to ensure adherence to best practices and alignment with client objectives.
  • Conduct thorough QA checks on Google Ad campaigns, including ad copy, targeting settings, landing page functionality, and tracking parameters.
  • Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams including digital specialists, customer experience, and other departments to identify and promptly resolve QA issues.
  • Act as a subject matter expert on QA best practices, tools, and methodologies within the digital marketing domain.
  • Maintain QA documentation, including test plans, test cases, and QA reports, to track and communicate QA progress and findings.

Qualifications

Essential:

  • Proven experience (3+ years) as a Test Analyst with a focus on test automation.
  • Strong proficiency in Azure DevOps for test case management and reporting.
  • Experience with k6 and Cypress testing tools.
  • Solid understanding of SaaS application testing methodologies.

Preferable:

  • Familiarity with Agile and Scrum methodologies.
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Strong communication and collaboration abilities.
